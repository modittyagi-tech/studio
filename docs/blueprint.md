# **App Name**: Glampify

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Visually stunning full-screen hero section with headline, subheadline, and primary CTA.
- Experience Overview: Emotional storytelling section highlighting unique glamping experiences.
- Featured Stays: Showcase curated glamping stays, ready to integrate with Supabase data.
- Luxury Highlights: Visually appealing section emphasizing luxury amenities and key features. 
- Booking Enquiry: Allow potential customers to book reservations or gather information using contact forms that send directly to Supabase
- Image Harmonizer: Leverage AI tool to ensures all uploaded images are automatically adjusted to fit the site's color palette. AI will suggest image modification if it's wildly out of sync, allowing the business owner to curate a harmonious visual theme with less effort.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest Green (#228B22) to evoke nature and tranquility.
- Background color: Cream (#F8F8FF), a desaturated near-white, for spaciousness and elegance.
- Accent color: Beige (#F5F5DC) to provide subtle contrast and highlight key elements.
- Body font: 'Alegreya', a serif font for a warm, classic, refined tone in longer blocks of text
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a sans-serif font with an elegant style suited to fashion, art, and design
- Subtle fade and slide-in animations with Framer Motion to enhance user experience without being distracting.
- Spacious and calm layout with generous whitespace, adhering to luxury design principles and mobile-first approach.
